If you're looking for someone who can fulfill your artistic style, you're in the right place. I create paintings, portraits and digital art. We can discuss and customize each project according to your vision.

Art is the best way to immortalize something beautiful, and I always strive for quality. There are no limits to creativity, and as a result, my portfolio continues to grow so that everyone can find something that resonates with them.

In the store, you can find ready-made artworks that are available for immediate shipping. However, I also create custom pieces. If you are interested in a custom artwork, please contact me via email at rubylissaart@gmail.com. We can discuss the dimensions and the medium for the portrait. I will provide you with a price quote in advance, as well as an estimated turnaround time for the completion of the artwork.
